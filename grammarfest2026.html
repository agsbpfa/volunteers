GrammarFest 2026
Volunteer Guide

Because even superheroes need a briefing 🦸

Hello, Amazing Volunteers! 👋

First of all, a massive THANK YOU for signing up to help run GrammarFest this Saturday! 🥳 It's going to be an absolute blast, and we'll be raising some much-needed funds for our fantastic boys at school.

I've put together these FAQs to answer the most common questions. Tap any section to expand it. If anything else comes up, don't hesitate to ping me on email or WhatsApp — I'm here to help! 💌

On Arrival

📍 Where Do I Start?

Look out for a friendly sign near the entrance directing you to collect your VOLUNTEER badges! 🌟 Wear yours proudly — it shows the world just how much this event depends on brilliant people like you.

Your badge will show your name, stall location, and timings. As a thank you, you'll also receive free tokens 🎁 — and yes, the bar takes tokens too!

Please aim to arrive at your stall at least 5–10 minutes before your session begins to keep things running smoothly. ⏰

Heads up: There's an adjoining event on the Astro turf until 5pm, so parking will be tricky. Allow extra time — and if there's a queue at the gate, let the staff know you're a volunteer and come straight in!

📍
Running Your Stall

🎪 What Do I Do At The Stall?

Don't worry — each stall has clear price lists and instructions so you can hit the ground running. ✅

Here's a quick payment guide so you know exactly what to expect at your stall:

💵 Cash accepted: Entrance · Token Stalls · Bar

💳 Card/Contactless accepted: Entrance · Token Stalls · Bar · Food Stalls · Cakes & Ice Creams · Zorb Football

🎟️ Tokens only (all other stalls): Please treat tokens exactly like cash — collect every single one in the box provided at your stall. They will all be counted at the end of the day, so don't let any go astray! 📊

Card payments are handled via SumUp machines connected to school WiFi — if one plays up, call me straight away! 📞

🎪
Tickets

🎟️ Do I Have to Buy Entrance Tickets?

Yes please! We kindly ask everyone attending — including wonderful volunteers and their families — to purchase tickets. Even the Headmaster, Mr. Wright, has bought his! 😉

Think of your free tokens as a VIP fast-pass to the best bits of the fair — a cold one at the bar or something from the World Food Court is absolutely calling your name! 🌍🍜

Tickets: hopp.to/agsb

🎟️
Can't Make It?

🚨 Last Minute Cancellation?

We'd absolutely love to see each and every one of you — this event only works because of your help! If something unexpected comes up, please phone me (WhatsApp call preferred) so we can arrange a backup quickly. 🤝

Cancellations without notice can cause real confusion on the day, so we're counting on your support. 🙏

🚨
Finishing Up

📸 Done Volunteering — What Next?

Please make sure the token tub, SumUp machine, and any cash float are securely handed to the next volunteer (or to Ravi if you're finishing at 6pm). 🔒

There's a VOLUNTEER GROUP PHOTO in the QUAD (near the music!) at 6 PM! 🥳 If you volunteered in the 3pm slot, please stick around and join us!

By appearing in the photo you're consenting for it to be used on PFA social media, the Headmaster's newsletter, and our FB, Instagram, and Twitter pages. Say cheese! 📸

📸
Eco Corner

♻️ Do I Keep The Volunteer Badge?

Please drop your badge back at the entrance on your way out — we reuse them for future PFA events. Thank you for helping us stay eco-friendly! 🌱
